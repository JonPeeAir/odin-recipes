Sweetroll

Description

Let me guess, You're dying to steal someone's sweetroll

Well look no further, just make one yourself

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup caster sugar
  • 1 cup maple syrup of honey
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 60g butter, melted
  • 1 vanilla pod, scraped
  • 3 tsp cinnamon powder

For glaze:

  • 1 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup icing sugar
  • 1 vanilla pod, scraped

You will also need:

  • 1 large bundt tin (or 2 smaller ones)
